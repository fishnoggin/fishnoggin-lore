Planesrift - The Knowing

Planesrift has never been able to sustain a centralized thieves' guild, each attempt dissolving into quibbling factions. In lieu of this, an alternative has emerged in recent years: The Knowing, an information-obsessed organization that serves to mediate between career criminals and those seeking their services.

As the story begins, you have received a letter from the Knowing. It beckons you to meet at a playhouse known as The Cradle for a job.

Operations

The Knowing have no outwardly clear goal of their own, save for knowing as much as they can about what happens within the walls of Planesrift. They do not seem to interact with the city on their own, but only through hired intermediaries.
