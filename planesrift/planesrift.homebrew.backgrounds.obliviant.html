Obliviant of Mirthark

By chance or fate, you have managed to become an adherent to the ways of Mirthark, God of the Obscure. Largely left to your own devices, you are tasked with keeping dangerous information safely away from those it might harm.
You might follow him in the interest of the greater good, for the privilege of accessing the information he guards, or simply because you enjoy living your life as a ghost.

Skill Proficiencies:

Choose two from Deception, Insight, Persuasion, or Stealth.

Tool Proficiencies:

One type of artisan’s tools or musical instrument of your choice.

Languages:

One exotic language of your choice.

Equipment:

A set of traveler’s clothes, a hooded cloak, a priest’s pack or explorer’s pack, a fogsbreath dagger, and a pouch containing 5gp.

Choose one feature from the following. Additional features may be granted later as boons.

Blanket of Ignorance:

In your service of Mirthark, you’ve discovered that some things are better left unknown, leaning on the God of Obscurity to suppress your curiosity towards dangers that would otherwise draw your attention.
When you are forced to make a Wisdom saving throw against an effect caused by something you can see or hear, you may use your reaction to give yourself advantage on the roll. You may use this feature twice and regain all uses of it when you complete a long rest.

Expanded Spell List:

As you gain levels, you are granted access to additional spells on your spell list(s). The spells are always prepared and do not count against your number of spells known.

Level: Spells:
1st Encode Thoughts, Protection from Evil and Good
3rd Nystul’s Magic Aura, Pass Without Trace
5th Intellect Fortress, Nondetection
7th Death Ward, Mordenkainen’s Private Sanctum
9th Mislead, Modify Memory

Wanderer's Shroud

The influence of Mirthark has made it difficult for even Death to find you. For every two years that pass, you age only one.
When you roll a death saving throw, you may add your Charisma (Stealth) modifier to the roll. Additionally, you treat a d20 roll of 18 or higher as a 20 when making a death saving throw.
