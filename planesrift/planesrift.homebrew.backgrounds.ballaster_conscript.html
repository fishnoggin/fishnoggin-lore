Planesrift - Backgrounds

Ballaster Conscript

You are a member of Planesrift’s newest criminal enterprise workers’ union. You might have turned to their employ after spending years as an exploited shiphand or overworked enchanter, been “recruited” after having your sorcerous talent noticed by one of their members, or joined as a tinkerer looking to make a name for yourself and your work.
Ballasters are typically sorcerers, artificers, wizards, rogues, or fighters, but all are welcome in their ranks. As a whole, the organization trends Chaotic Neutral.

Skill Proficiencies

Choose two from Acrobatics, Arcana, or Persuasion.
