Planesrift: Characters

Emíl Gestalt

Alignment: Lawful Evil

Affiliations: Red Hunt

Psyche:   -2

The current Archhunter of the Red Hunt, Emíl Gestalt has marked a recent shift in the organization's direction. A changeling himself, Gestalt has opened the group slightly as a refuge to those nonhumans rejected by the Chapel. This supposes, however, that the individuals in question are able to survive life in the Hunt, which is rarely the case.

Like many other changlings in Planesrift, Gestalt came into the city as an illegal slave and, like many others, is missing two fingers on his right hand - the comparatively lenient punishment in the city for a slave killing their master.

Though his skin is disfigured in many places and his breathing constantly labored, the Archhunter's office is one earned by violence - an office that is sufficient proof in itself of the unpredictable danger he may pose to outsiders.
