Planesrift: Characters

Aldo Garron

Alignment: Lawful Good

Affiliations: Justiciars

Psyche:   +3

A stickler for the law, Garron is an ambitious Inspector for the Justiciar's office that has proven himself a constant thorn in the side of nearly every criminal in Planesrift. He is known for his distaste for magic and adherence to legalism, sometimes even when it precludes his own morality.

Aldo Garron is the human disguise of Enthryngil, the golden dragon to which the Ordathrens have sworn allegiance. A curse or injury of some sort keeps him in human form while he searches for a cure.

Points of Relevance:

•   Expert-level Martial Combat practitioner and trainer

•   Master-level Evocation magic, Abjuration magic, and Drakewarden trainer.
