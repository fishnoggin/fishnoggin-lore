Red Hunt Allegiant

You’ve chosen to throw your lot in with the Red Hunt, an esoteric organization dedicated to the pursuit of self-discipline and indomitable power. You might spend your time poring over ancient texts to divine the future, working as a blade-for-hire to hone your skills, or cutting your own way through Planesrift’s nighttime streets.
Regardless of how deeply you subscribe to the gang’s beliefs, the operations of the entire organization are underlined by a singular fear: the Night of Last Hunt is coming, and you must be strong enough to come out on top when it does.
Members of the Red Hunt are commonly rangers, warlocks, rogues, fighters, and occasionally evil-aligned paladins or clerics. As a whole, the organization trends Lawful Evil.

Skill Proficiencies:

Choose two from Insight, Intimidation, or Stealth.

Tool Proficiencies:

Choose from thieves’ tools or one type of simple or martial weapon.

Languages:

One exotic language of your choice.

Equipment:

One simple or martial weapon with which you are proficient, a set of traveler’s clothes, a pouch containing 5gp, a book or tablet containing the Red Hunt’s Account of Days, and a finely-embroidered red sash or scarf.

Hunter's Sight:

You learn your choice of either the Hex or Hunter’s Mark spells and may cast that spell once at 1st level without spending a spell slot or using material components. You regain the ability to cast the spell in this way after you complete a long rest. While you have another creature under the effect of a spell cast using this feature, you gain darkvision out to a range of 60 feet. If you already have darkvision, the range instead increases by 60 feet.
